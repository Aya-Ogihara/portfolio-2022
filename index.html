About Me

Welcome to my page! I am Aya Ogihara. I am a front-end developer with a background in customer service and I am currently available for full-time work in Auckland area or remotely.

When I was in Japan I was also a kimono dressmaker and was deeply interested in Japanese tradition. Moving abroad, my interest has shifted from the tradition to the future, and I am fascinated by the wonderfulness of technology, which is a symbol of the future.
I have an artisanal spirit and I am interested in front-end development that allows bringing both the technical and visual aspects of digital products to life, and I enjoy keeping up to date with the continuous changes in the world of technology.

I consider myself an advocate for change with an open-minded to ideas for further growth, I am also excellent at collaborating with stakeholders, clients, and designers and enjoy working in a highly collaborative and supportive work environment.
